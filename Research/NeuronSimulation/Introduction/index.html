Introduction

NEURON is a programming environment for simulating neuronal activity and more. There is a big community among research institutes and on the internet.

My projects are focusing on hyperpolarization-activated cation channels (HCN) and Kv1.1 channels, which contribute to Ih and ID, respectively. I organized some resources and my notes here. I hope you might find them useful.
